Facts & Questions

What does it mean to be a ​Campus Ambassador?

As a College Ambassador, you will be the representative of Wings at your institution. You will be the first point of contact for any student who requires information about Wings and you’ll be responsible for assisting them as and when needed.

Am I eligible to be a ​Campus Ambassador?

To be eligible, all you need is a valid student id and a lot of enthusiasm.

How can I apply for ​Campus Ambassador?

Click on the register button to apply now! Once the registration is complete, you will not be able to change your email address. Allow pop-ups if prompted to do so.

What are my responsibilities as a Campus Ambassador?

As a College Ambassador, you will be required to share Wings, GECA posts on all social media platforms and put up posters on your college notice boards. You will also be instrumental in conducting workshops and sessions under the aegis of Wings ,GECA

Can there be multiple ​Campus Ambassadors from a single college?

Yes, depending on the size of the college, Wings GECA can appoint multiple College Ambassadors.
